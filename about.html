Explore Us Before We Explore You

Half the world denies our existence, the other half continues to search for us. But rest assured, we will come to you when you least expect it! We are there to find the answers you need and we need to know.

What to Expect for your Appointment:

You will be escorted to our facility to meet your big-eyed guide. Your guide will ensure your comfort for your stay. Come in open-minded, leave enlightened!

What to Bring:

Nothing! We will supply all your needs.

Possible Side Effects:

While there are some potential side effects, don't let them dissuade you from visiting - you don't have a choice anyways.

  • Soreness and tenderness for up to one week after returning home
  • Minor confusion
    • Avoid treating confusion by consulting family & friends - they won't understand and may have you committed
    • If the men in black suits visit, don't tell them a thing
  • Loss of identity - Don't worry though, you had little to begin with
    • Be advised, loss of identity may last 6 hours to 35 years

History of Us:

We are a fast-growing company that began in the small town of Roswell, New Mexico where we crash landed in 1947. There we began serving the local community, against their conscious will. Eventually, we were well accepted and made a main part of the local economy. And now we are here to serve YOU in finding answers!
